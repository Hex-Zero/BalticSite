About Baltic

Our vision is to champion change and to constantly look to the future. To transform the way people recruit, to change the way people learn and to positively shift the perception of apprenticeships. Ultimately, we change lives.

About Baltic

Baltic’s specialist apprenticeship programmes launch careers in IT, software development and digital marketing. We were the first training provider to offer a completely digital apprenticeship training platform, and we haven’t looked back since.

We are dedicated, digital experts who are passionate about making a recognised social impact. Regardless of race, gender, location and economic status, our innovative approach to technical training is bringing a host of opportunities to those who may have previously considered a career in tech to be out of reach.

Value 1:

Change is Good

Our belief in change is at the heart of everything we do. Whether we’re changing the futures of young people or transforming the way modern businesses recruit, it’s our adaptability that gives us an edge.
Value 2:

Digitally Dedicated

A passion for technology is the driving force behind the learning experience we offer. Through our SMART Classroom training, we’re bridging the skills gap and encouraging collaboration across the UK’s thriving tech sector.
Value 3:

Heroic Support

Our selfless approach to customer service is what sets us apart. Our staff aren’t just dependable, they are heroic. This culture of care allows us to exceed expectations at every stage of the apprenticeship journey.
