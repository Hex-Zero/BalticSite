Baltic Apprenticeships

If you’re looking to turn your hobby into a career, apprenticeships are a great way to get the experience and qualifications you need. If you love computers and technology, our Infrastructure apprenticeships could be the path for you. If you’re social media obsessed, our Digital Marketing programme could be the route to your dream career. Or, if you enjoy gaming and coding, our Software Development apprenticeship could help you achieve your goals.

We are dedicated to making sure you have the best possible technical training, allowing you to develop the knowledge needed to thrive in a digital role. With the support of the fantastic employers we work with, you will gain real industry experience and invaluable practical skills which will put you well on your way to a career in tech.

How do apprenticeships work?

Our digital apprenticeships provide the ideal blend of technical training and hands on industry experience. You’ll be employed in a real job while studying to achieve an industry recognised qualification, all while earning your own wage!

Through our interactive SMART Classroom, you’ll receive in-depth training that is relevant to your role and industry. By the end of your apprenticeship, you’ll have all the skills you need to take the next steps in your career.

As well as technical knowledge, you’ll also gain a whole host of workplace skills that will set you up for life. Transferable human skills such as communication, teamwork, problem-solving and creativity will give you to confidence to go far in your chosen field.
